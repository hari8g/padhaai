Fundraise

Paadhai gets active support from its generous donors. We normally identify requirements, define the project and then look for funds. Hence, we need to be effective to be able to raise the money needed to implement the envisaged project.

Helping us raise funds, at short notices, would be of immense help. You can assist Paadhai there, with your personal contacts, business or professional network or through CSR initiatives of organizations. Paadhai also has a corpus, so as to address urgent needs.
