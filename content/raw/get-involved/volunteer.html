Volunteers

Paadhai is nothing without its volunteers. We are an NGO that has no employees and are totally supported, by our volunteer network only.

Paadhai’s volunteers come from different backgrounds and are spread across the globe. This enables us to handle projects in different geographies

How all can you get involved ? You can teach or train students and communities, run projects, assist in fund raising, network volunteers, spread our message, donate funds or be a well wisher. We welcome any kind participation. Choose your way of working with us.
