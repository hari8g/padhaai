Paadhai, the way...

Paadhai works as a catalyst organization to bridge gaps in social and community service. We connect donors and impoverished groups - ensure that worthy projects get the deserved attention and all funds are well applied.

In early 2005, following the world wide shock of the Asian Tsunami, a small group of spirited and like- minded people from various parts of the world came together. They strongly desired to make a difference in the lives of impoverished ones. Not by just feeding them but by educating and training them, empowering them and increasing their self reliance â€“ by showing them the way.

With humble beginnings and high ideals, "Paadhai" was thus born !
