Our People

Countless! That is the number of people who work behind the scenes for the most part. They directly and indirectly contribute to its existence and successful functioning. They continue to do every bit in their capacity and lead others on “the path” shining with the promise of a fruitful today and an abundant tomorrow!

We acknowledge dozens of volunteers, across all ages, from different parts of the world, who participate in all Paadhai activities. These volunteers are the essence of Paadhai and are, truly, our lifeline.
