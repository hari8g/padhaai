Education & Training

Education not only provides knowledge, but also serves as a gateway for financial sustenance. PAADHAI holds this view to heart and works towards building a sustainable community, especially in remote villages and tribal areas. This helps the process of integration of these groups of people into mainstream society.

We have been involved in setting up education and training centers in remote and tribal areas by providing the required infrastructure such as buildings, housing, libraries, science laboratories, and transportation. Study materials have also been made available to students through fund acquisition. These goals have been implemented by working in tandem with associate NGOs to provide the necessary teaching staff and workforce.

Paadhai is also involved in content creation and mentoring activities that handholds teachers, schools and communities in effectively educating students at various levels. (A short video on our Educational projects)
